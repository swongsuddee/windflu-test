Source:

Scope: authenticated brand create-campaign coverage on PROF, split by phase
for campaign form and preview, date validation, payment selection, and the
brand-side QR success handoff.

Confidence level: 97%

## Coverage Summary

- The live create-campaign route is an authenticated multi-phase wizard at
  `/brand/create-campaign`.
- Phase 1 covers campaign form and preview behavior only.
- Phase 1.1 image cropping and adjustment is intentionally deferred for now.
- Phase 2 covers campaign start-end date validation and valid progression.
- Phase 3 covers campaign payment selection with QR-only as the supported
  success-path payment method for this design.
- Admin approval after QR payment is out of scope; this module stops at the
  brand-side success/handoff state.

## Deferred Coverage

- Phase 1.1 image cropping and adjustment remains intentionally skipped for
  this batch.
- Admin approval and any post-QR internal review flow remain out of scope.

## Recording Rule

Successful brand create-campaign runs should add a new row to the local
campaign registry at `src/test-data/created-campaigns.md`.

This local registry is gitignored and should store only safe campaign metadata
needed for QA traceability, such as created time, flow ID, campaign title, and
notes. Do not record auth tokens, cookies, or payment secrets in that file.
